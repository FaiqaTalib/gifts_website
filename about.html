Our Story

Turning Gifts Into Golden Memories

VelvetWrap was born in 2018 from a simple belief: every gift should feel as special as the person receiving it. Founded by Fatima Noor in Karachi, what started as a small boutique wrapping service has grown into Pakistan's most beloved premium gifting destination.

We partner with over 50 local and international brands to bring you the most curated selection of luxury gifts — from handcrafted hampers and artisanal chocolates to bespoke jewellery and personalized keepsakes. Every VelvetWrap package is prepared with care, delivered with love.
