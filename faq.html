Got Questions?

We Have Answers

Everything you need to know about ordering from VelvetWrap

Orders & Delivery
How long does delivery take?
+
For Karachi orders placed before 2PM, we offer same-day delivery. Standard delivery across major cities takes 2-3 business days. Express next-day delivery is available for an additional PKR 350.
Is gift wrapping included in all orders?
+
Yes! All VelvetWrap orders come with our signature premium gift wrapping at no extra charge. You can also opt for our luxury velvet box upgrade at checkout for an additional PKR 500.
Do you deliver nationwide in Pakistan?
+
Absolutely! We deliver to all major cities including Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan, Peshawar, and more. Remote areas may take 4-5 business days.
Can I track my order?
+
Yes! Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email. You can also contact our support team at any time for live order updates.
Payments
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), bank transfers, EasyPaisa, JazzCash, and all major credit/debit cards via our secure payment gateway.
Is online payment secure?
+
Yes, all online transactions are processed through SSL-encrypted, PCI-compliant payment gateways. Your card details are never stored on our servers.
Do you offer installment payments?
+
Yes! We offer 0% installment plans for orders above PKR 10,000 through select bank cards. Contact our team for details on eligible banks and plans.
Returns & Refunds
What is your return policy?
+
We offer a 7-day hassle-free return policy on all unopened, undamaged items. Perishable products (chocolates, fresh flowers, food items) are non-returnable for hygiene reasons.
My item arrived damaged. What do I do?
+
We're so sorry to hear that! Please take a photo and contact us within 24 hours of delivery via WhatsApp or email. We'll arrange a free replacement or full refund promptly.
How long does a refund take?
+
Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed within 3–5 business days to your original payment method.
Customization & Gifting
Can I add a personalized message to my gift?
+
Absolutely! Every order includes a complimentary handwritten message card. Simply enter your message during checkout and our team will include it beautifully presented in the box.
Do you offer corporate bulk gifting?
+
Yes! We specialize in corporate gifting packages with custom branding, logo printing, and bulk discounts starting from 20 units. Contact us at corporate@velvetwrap.com for custom quotes.
Can I build a custom hamper?
+
Yes! Our "Build Your Own Hamper" service lets you choose your preferred items, box size, ribbon color, and message card. Available on our Shop page or contact us directly.
